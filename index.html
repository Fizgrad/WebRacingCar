Build your rally track

  1. Click empty ground to add key points.
  2. Drag blue/red/green points to reshape the track.
  3. Use the side panel to adjust curve smoothness.

Press Enter or click Start to drive.
Use the mouse wheel to zoom the top-down view.

Green = start, red = end. More points give better control over straight and arc sections.
