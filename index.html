Draw your rally track

  1. Press and hold the left mouse button on the ground.
  2. Drag to sketch the path your car will follow. Smooth, flowing curves drive best.
  3. Release the mouse when you reach the finish line.

Press Enter to build the track and start driving.
Use the mouse wheel to zoom the top-down view.
Drawing again will replace your previous path.

Tip: keep curves wide. Tight hairpins are hard to take at speed.

Tilt to steer

Hold your phone in landscape and tilt left/right to steer. We need your permission to read the device's tilt sensors.
